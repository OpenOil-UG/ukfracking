UK Fracking

The UK government (August 2015) has awarded some licenses for fracking onshore and made recommendations for more, subject to the results of a public consultation on environmental considerations. Find out if you live in or near where a license may be granted – zoom in, or just type your postcode in the search bar.


Blocks Awarded. 27 blocks which have awarded by the government. Operators still need approval from local authorities in these areas to go ahead
Blocks Under Consultation The 132 blocks which have been recommended for award by the government – pending public consultation on environmental effects due to run until the end of September.
Blocks Tendered All 1168 blocks which were tendered in the 14th onshore bidding round
